! COMMON MISTAKE
Drawing S/R on 1m, 5m, or 15m charts. Those levels are noise - use them only for entries, never for S/R identification.
SUPPORT & RESISTANCE - ZONES NOT LINES
Markets rarely reverse at an exact price. Use zones:
X Resistance = exactly $100
OK Resistance = $98-$101 zone

.Site of previous breakouts or breakdowns
.Associated with high volume spikes

🕯 CANDLESTICK PATTERNS
BULLISH - For Buys
Bullish Engulfing
Large green candle completely engulfs the prior bearish candle
Hammer
Long lower wick, small body - buyers rejected lower prices
Morning Star
Bearish candle -> small indecision candle -> strong bullish candle
BEARISH - For Sells
Bearish Engulfing
Large bearish candle engulfs the prior bullish candle
Shooting Star
Long upper wick - sellers rejected higher prices
Evening Star
Three-candle bearish reversal - opposite of Morning Star
